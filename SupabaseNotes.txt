## How PowerSync Actually Fits In

Think of the architecture like this:

```text
Flutter / React App
        │
        ▼
    PowerSync
   (local SQLite)
        │
        ▼
    PowerSync Sync
        │
        ▼
     Supabase
   (Postgres)
```

Your app talks primarily to **PowerSync's local database**.

PowerSync then:

* downloads data from Supabase
* stores it locally in SQLite
* syncs changes when online
* gives offline-first behavior

This is why PowerSync is popular for task managers, CRMs, field-service apps, and banking-style mobile applications where offline access matters.
